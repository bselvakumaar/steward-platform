# StockSteward AI: Executive Manual
## Role: Business Owner / Executive

Welcome to the **Executive Dashboard**. Your view provides high-level business intelligence, asset ROI, and treasury monitoring.

### 1. High-Level Metrics
- **Total Managed Assets (TMA)**: The aggregate value of all sub-portfolios under management.
- **Portfolio ROI**: A bird's-eye view of net returns across the entire organization.
- **Revenue Monitoring**: Tracking management fees and performance-based incentive growth.

### 2. Strategic Insights
- **Agent Intelligence**: Read high-level summaries of the AI's current market sentiment and proposed rebalancing.
- **Market Movers**: Monitor top-performing assets that are driving the firm's growth.

### 3. Subscription Management
- **Upgrade Path**: Manage Enterprise SLAs, White-label reporting options, and DMA (Direct Market Access) configurations.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Executive Dashboard is designed for decision support and capital allocation, not granular trade execution.
