## Overview

Welcome to the StockSteward AI Trader User Manual. This guide will help you navigate and utilize all features available to traders within the platform. As a trader, you have access to advanced trading tools, real-time market data, AI-powered insights, and portfolio management features.

### Role Permissions
- View and execute trades
- Access to portfolio information
- View market data and analytics
- Limited administrative functions
- Access to trading history and performance reports

## Getting Started

### Account Setup
1. **Login**: Navigate to the StockSteward AI platform and enter your credentials
2. **Profile Verification**: Complete your profile with trading preferences
3. **Risk Tolerance**: Set your risk tolerance level (Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive)
4. **Trading Mode**: Choose between Auto (AI-assisted) or Manual trading

### Initial Dashboard Tour
- **Market Overview**: Real-time market data and trending stocks
- **Portfolio Summary**: Current holdings and performance metrics
- **Quick Actions**: Place trades, view watchlist, access reports
- **AI Insights**: AI-generated trading recommendations

## Trading Features

### Placing Trades
1. **Select Symbol**: Search for the stock symbol you want to trade
2. **Choose Action**: Select BUY or SELL
3. **Set Quantity**: Enter the number of shares
4. **Order Type**: Choose from:
   - Market Order: Execute at current market price
   - Limit Order: Execute at specified price
   - Stop Loss: Sell when price drops to specified level
   - Trailing Stop: Dynamic stop loss that adjusts with price movement
5. **Review & Confirm**: Verify all details before placing order

### Advanced Trading Options
- **Position Sizing**: AI suggests optimal position size based on risk tolerance
- **Risk Analysis**: View potential risk/reward before executing trade
- **Technical Indicators**: Access to RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, etc.
- **AI Recommendations**: AI-generated buy/sell signals with rationale

### Trade History
- **Recent Trades**: View your last 10 executed trades
- **Trade Details**: Access to price, fees, and performance metrics
- **Export Options**: Download trade history as CSV

## Risk Management

### Setting Risk Parameters
1. **Stop Loss Orders**: Automatically sell if price drops below threshold
2. **Position Limits**: Maximum percentage of portfolio per stock
3. **Daily Loss Limits**: Stop trading if daily losses exceed threshold
4. **Sector Exposure**: Limit exposure to specific sectors

### Risk Monitoring

- **Volatility Tracking**: Monitor portfolio volatility over time
