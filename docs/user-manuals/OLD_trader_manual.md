# StockSteward AI: Trader / Investor Manual
## Role: Active Portfolio Manager

Welcome to the **Trading Hub**. Your role focuses on asset growth, execution efficiency, and AI-assisted alpha generation.

### 1. Portfolio Management
- **Real-time Metrics**: Monitor Total Equity, Daily Alpha, and Win Rate.
- **Exposure Control**: Review open positions and their weighted risk in the portfolio.
- **Equity Curve**: Analyze historical performance against benchmarks like the S&P 500.

### 2. Trading Hub
- **Autonomous Execution**: Deploy the AI Steward to manage entries and exits based on predefined strategies.
- **Strategic Overlap**: Combine multiple strategies to hedge against market volatility.
- **Manual Overrides**: Pause or resume the Agent at any time for manual intervention.

### 3. Reports & Analytics
- **Performance Summaries**: Export daily, weekly, or yearly performance PDF reports.
- **Tax Harvesting**: Use the intelligence engine to identify tax-loss harvesting opportunities.
