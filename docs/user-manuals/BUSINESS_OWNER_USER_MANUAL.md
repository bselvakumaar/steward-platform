## Overview

Welcome to the StockSteward AI Business Owner User Manual. This guide provides comprehensive instructions for business owners to manage their investment operations, oversee team activities, and monitor business performance. As a business owner, you have elevated access to financial data, user management, and strategic analytics.

### Role Permissions
- Full access to portfolio and trading data
- User management capabilities
- Financial reporting and analytics
- Strategic planning tools
- Compliance oversight
- Risk management controls
- Administrative functions (limited)
